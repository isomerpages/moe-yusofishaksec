<h3>Student’s MIMS password reset</h3>
<p>To reset your MIMS password:</p>
<p>1. Use the self-service option: Click "Forgot Password?" on the MIMS login
page, enter your username (iCON email address), and answer the challenge
questions you previously set up.</p>
<p>2. If self-service doesn't work: Visit the school's General Office for
assistance. This applies if you haven't set up challenge questions or are
unable to answer them correctly.</p>
